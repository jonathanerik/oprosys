ONLINE PROCUREMENT SYSTEM

ONLINE PROCUREMENT SYSTEM is a purchase request system online-documented to request any items needed by the organization. Traditionally, the request forms are written in a certain paper/form and then passed on to departments for evaluations. Upon submitting the request form to the Purchasing Department will the form will be generated into a Purchase Order. With the use of the system, the paper and written documents will be eliminated thus it will be online-documented. Using the system, the request forms will be precise and easier to manage.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why cant I open the system?
- Make sure you are connected to the internet because the system is an online based and can only be access with an ineternet connection
2. What is minimum requirements to access the system?
- Your Desktop must at least have intel celeron, 4 GB RAM, and 500GB HDD
-Your Windows version must be Windows 7 or higher
- It can be access using Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome
3. How do I create an account?
- To create an account, you need to contact the ICTC office and set up an account for you to use
4. How do I create a request form?
- To create a request form, simply login to your account (contact ICTC if you dont have an account) then create form. Fill out the necessary fields and then submit it to your immediate superior.
5. How will I know if my request is approved or not?
- Simple Log in to your account and open the list of your request, in the table of your requests, you can confirm at the status column whether your requests is approved or not. In addition you could also review the comments of the ICTC section
